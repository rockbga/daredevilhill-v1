About Us

Built to Make
Something Real

DareDevilHill is an independent production company based in Burbank, California, dedicated to developing and producing original content that is fresh, fearless, and cinematic.

We believe the best stories come from a place of daring — creatively and technically. Whether we're developing our own films or partnering with fellow creatives to bring their vision to life, we bring the same relentless craft and collaborative spirit to every project.

From concept to screen, we offer production services for filmmakers, brands, and storytellers who refuse to settle for ordinary.

Michael Beeson
Founder · Director · Screenwriter · Producer · US Air Force Veteran

Michael Beeson is the founder and creative director of DareDevilHill. A US Air Force veteran, he brings discipline and a singular creative voice to every project. Before launching DareDevilHill, he spent nearly a decade at Warner Bros. on campaigns for Wonder Woman, Dunkirk, and Detective Pikachu, with additional roots at DreamWorks Animation and CAA.

Michael grew up in Anchorage, Alaska — and those stories, that landscape, and that community have never left him. He makes films rooted in that spirit, and believes deeply in the power of cinema to give voice to places and people that don't always get to see themselves on screen. DareDevilHill exists not just to tell Michael's stories, but to help other filmmakers and creatives bring their own to life.
