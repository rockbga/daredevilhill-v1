About Us

Built to Make
Something Real

DareDevilHill is an independent production company based in Burbank, California, dedicated to developing and producing original content that is fresh, fearless, and cinematic.

We believe the best stories come from a place of daring — creatively and technically. Whether we're developing our own films or partnering with fellow creatives to bring their vision to life, we bring the same relentless craft and collaborative spirit to every project.

From concept to screen, we offer production services for filmmakers, brands, and storytellers who refuse to settle for ordinary.

Michael Beeson
Director · Screenwriter · Producer · Veteran

Michael Beeson is the founder and creative director of DareDevilHill. A US Air Force Academy graduate, he brings discipline and a singular creative voice to every project. Before launching DareDevilHill, he spent nearly a decade at Warner Bros. on campaigns for Wonder Woman, Dunkirk, Detective Pikachu, and more — and has roots at DreamWorks Animation and CAA.

🎖 USAF Veteran Warner Bros. DreamWorks Animation CAA Alumni
